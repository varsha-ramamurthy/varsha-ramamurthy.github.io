Latitude vs. Wind Speeds



Observations


The overall wind speed in different cities across the world appears to be low. The highest wind speed is between 35-40 mph in the +10 - +20 range. This is close to the equator near the Tropic of Cancer. The next highest wind spped plot is observed to be above 25 mph in the Tropic of Capricorn. The average wind speed in the northern hemisphere is 5-10 mph.
